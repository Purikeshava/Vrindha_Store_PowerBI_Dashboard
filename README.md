# 📊 Vrindha Store Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** developed using the **Vrindha Store Sales Dataset**. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional performance through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify key performance indicators and growth opportunities.

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which states contribute the most sales?
- What is the preferred payment method?
- Which customer segment drives the business?
- How do monthly sales trends change over time?
- Which categories require more business attention?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Design an interactive business dashboard
- Track overall sales performance
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior

# 📌 Business Insights

### 📍 Sales Performance

- Identified peak sales months and seasonal demand patterns.
- Compared monthly revenue trends to evaluate business growth.

### 📍 Product Analysis

- Determined the best-selling product categories.
- Identified products generating maximum revenue.

### 📍 Regional Analysis

- Compared sales performance across different states.
- Identified top-performing and low-performing regions.

### 📍 Payment Analysis

- Analyzed customer payment preferences.
- Evaluated the most frequently used payment methods.

# 💡 Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing states.
- Improve promotions during low-sales months.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies for high-value customers.
- Optimize payment options preferred by customers.
- Monitor cancelled orders to improve operational efficiency.
